Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our leave management and wellbeing platform.

How do I apply for leave?

Simply log into your employee dashboard and click "Apply Leave" or use the "Quick Leave" button. Fill in your leave type (Sick, Vacation, or Personal), select your dates, and provide a reason. Your request will be sent to administrators for approval and you'll receive real-time status updates.

How can I check my leave balance?

Your current leave balances are displayed prominently on your dashboard. You can see your available Sick Leave (10 days), Vacation (15 days), and Personal Leave (5 days). If you need more days, you can request a balance increase directly from the dashboard.

What wellness features are available?

Our wellness center includes curated articles on mental health, work-life balance, and physical wellbeing. You can also register for wellness events like yoga sessions, workshops, and team building activities. All wellness content is accessible from your employee dashboard.

How do administrators manage leave requests?

Administrators have access to a comprehensive dashboard where they can view all pending leave requests, approve or reject them with comments, manage employee balances, and track leave patterns. They can also create wellness content and company notices.

Can I request additional leave days?

Yes! If you need more leave days, you can request a balance increase directly from your dashboard. Click "Request More" next to any leave type, provide a reason and the number of days needed. Administrators will review and approve balance increase requests.

How do I track my leave history?

Visit the "Leave History" page from your dashboard to see all your past and current leave requests. You can view the status (Pending, Approved, Rejected), dates, and any comments from administrators. Recent requests also appear on your main dashboard.
